Mantis Web Design

About

I'm a self-taught web developer from Lancaster, Pennsylvania. I got interested in web development and web design when I assisted a web developer to set up websites for nonprofits at a former job. From there I started learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. In my spare time I enjoy reading, hiking, playing around with the ukulele, and hanging out with my wife and two daughters.

services

I'm committed to providing my clients with a strong, unique web presence. I will work you to understand your business or organization's mission, vision, culture, and brand, so that your website shines! I offer:

  • Consulting on how to maximize the potential of your website for your marketing plan
  • A web presence customized to fit you. Whether you want simple and elegant or brash and daring, I build websites to fit your brand.
  • Responsive design so that whether people are viewing your website on a cell phone, tablet, or computer, your website will look great!
